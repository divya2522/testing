School Library Management Software

myly Library Management offers a fully functional yet easy to use solution to schools to automate their library.

Integrated with the myly school ERP, myly Library Management is the right choice for schools as their library software. It makes managing your library very easy. Add items, manage location in library, issue and receipt in a breeze. Integrated bar-code scanner makes the process of issue receipt even more convenient. Just point and click, and you are done. Allow parents and teachers to search for available library books from their app. Who needs another library app?

Features & Benefits

Library Item Management

Manage library items across categories like books, magazines, journals etc.

Library Stock Keeping

Inward new items and retire old items in an easy to use manner.

Bar-code Reader Integration

Integrated Honeywell bar-code reader to scan ISBN/ISSN codes in fraction of a second.

Item Location Management

Fetching and returning item from its defined location becomes very easy.

Item search from App

Parents and teachers can search if an item is in the library and its available quantity.

Reporting

Variety of reports like inward, retired items, return overdue and library collection.
